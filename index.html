About Me

I began my professional journey at the University of Central Florida in 2017, first working at the Faculty Center for Teaching and Learning as a Graphic Design Assistant. In 2021, I transitioned into Webcourses@UCF Support as an IT Client Support Specialist I.

I earned my Associate of Arts degree from Seminole State College of Florida in 2016, followed by a Bachelor of Arts in Digital Media: Game Design in 2020. I am currently pursuing my Master of Arts in Digital Media.

Throughout my career at UCF, I have supported users with Canvas, Microsoft Office, PeopleSoft, Windows, macOS, ChromeOS, and hardware troubleshooting for computers, tablets, printers, cameras, and microphones.

I have also worked in web development, video production, photography, digital art, and served as a teaching assistant for UCF’s Adobe Initiative. In June 2025, I joined the School of Communication Sciences and Disorders.

Outside of UCF, I freelance as a video editor and digital character artist. I also enjoy creating digital art, building games, and using Unreal Engine.

I hold certifications in 3D Printing and Interactive Media Design from Seminole State College of Florida.
